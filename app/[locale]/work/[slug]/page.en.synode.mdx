## üíé Inovations

- Synode uses a unique approach to handle transactions by leveraging Peer-2-Peer WiFi connections, eliminating the need for a centralized server.
- Avalanche Blockchain is used to secure transactions, ensuring that all data is tamper-proof and verifiable.
- The app is designed to work offline, allowing users to make transactions without an internet connection, which is a significant innovation in the realm of payment applications.
